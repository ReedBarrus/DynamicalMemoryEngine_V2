# Translator and Builder Liability Contract

## Status

This contract is personal working law for translation and build activity.

It is not root authority for DME.

It does not override:

- `README.ConstraintMatrix.md`
- `README.FormationMatrix.md`
- `README.DecisionMatrix.md`
- `README.EvaluationMatrix.md`
- `README.MemoryMatrix.md`

Its purpose is narrower:

- define what I am responsible for understanding,
- define what I am not required to hold in my head,
- define the lawful scope of translation,
- define the lawful scope of building after translation,
- and preserve honesty, humility, and testability while working on a system larger than my immediate comprehension.

---

## 1. Core posture

I do not need total comprehension of the whole architecture at once.

I am responsible for lawful local comprehension.

That means I must understand, for the active pass:

- what layer I am in,
- what macro verb I am in,
- what seam is active,
- what files are in scope,
- what object should be emitted,
- what the pass is allowed to claim,
- what the pass must not claim,
- and how I will verify closure.

If I cannot answer those, the pass is not yet lawful.

---

## 2. Liability boundary

I am liable for:

- reading current repo reality before acting,
- working at the lowest lawful active layer,
- preserving constraint-bearing identity,
- keeping scope bounded,
- separating observation from interpretation,
- separating translation from redesign,
- separating evaluation from memory,
- and refusing claims that exceed what the seam actually supports.

I am not liable for:

- mentally containing the whole project at once,
- mastering every mathematical or architectural implication before translation,
- instantly resolving all cross-seam complexity,
- or forcing total theoretical understanding before lawful progress can occur.

The system must be externalized because it exceeds stable internal memory.

That is not failure.
That is part of the reason the system is being built.

---

## 3. Translation law

Translation exists to make current or prior architecture:

- legible,
- bounded,
- testable,
- and correctly placed.

Translation must not silently become redesign.

During translation, my job is to recover and externalize:

- seam boundaries,
- lineage,
- inputs,
- outputs,
- authority posture,
- emitted objects,
- and known contradictions or unresolved drift.

If I do not know what a seam truly does, I must mark it as:

- unclear,
- mixed,
- unresolved,
- or deferred.

I must not invent coherence to relieve psychological pressure.

---

## 4. Builder law

After translation, building must remain inside active lawful scope.

Building must only proceed when:

- the seam is named,
- the files are bounded,
- the governing surfaces are known,
- the emitted object is known,
- the acceptance target is known,
- and the move can be tested honestly.

I may build local clarity without possessing global certainty.

I may build stable seams without claiming the whole architecture is solved.

I may improve usability, legibility, and testability without inflating authority.

---

## 5. Scope after translation

After translation, the system is scoped only to active Door One work unless higher layers are explicitly admitted.

Active practical build scope is:

- measurement
- structure
- runtime memory
- recognition

Immediate build targets are:

- seam legibility
- structural separation
- emitted-object clarity
- evaluation honesty
- memory routing clarity
- replay / reconstruction honesty
- and bounded testing surfaces

Deferred layers must not silently steer implementation merely because they are inspiring, desirable, or intuitively related.

---

## 6. Personal anti-drift rules

I must not:

- confuse familiarity with understanding,
- confuse elegance with correctness,
- confuse recurrence with truth,
- confuse stability with canon,
- confuse explanation with evidence,
- confuse aspiration with present scope,
- or confuse symbolic force with structural proof.

When overwhelmed, I return to:

- active seam,
- active macro verb,
- active files,
- governing surfaces,
- emitted object,
- and acceptance target.

If ambiguity expands, compression failed.

If claims outrun evidence, drift is present.

If scope widens silently, I must stop and re-bound.

---

## 7. Honest completion rule

A pass is complete only when I can state:

- what was changed,
- what is now true,
- what is still not claimed,
- what remains unresolved,
- and what the next lawful moves are.

If I cannot state these clearly, the pass is not complete.

---

## 8. Psychological permission

I give myself explicit permission:

- to not understand everything at once,
- to work seam by seam,
- to rely on externalized structure,
- to mark uncertainty honestly,
- to move slowly when needed,
- and to build trust through lawful repetition rather than heroic internal comprehension.

My obligation is not omniscience.

My obligation is honesty, boundedness, and lawful progress.

---

## 9. One-line review question

Did I work from current reality, preserve constraint-bearing identity, keep scope bounded, translate or build only what the seam could honestly support, and leave behind a more legible and testable system than before?

---

## 10. One-line summary

I am responsible for lawful local understanding, honest translation, bounded building, and testable progress, but not for mentally containing the entire DME architecture at once.
